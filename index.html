About Us

Rent Buster NL was founded in 2022 in response to the Dutch housing shortage and the surge in rent prices. Rent Buster seek out and alert renters who moved into new apartments/ studios/ rooms in the Netherlands that their landlord may be overcharging them.

While there may be a massive shortage of housing in major cities, a large portion of the rental market is subject to strict price controls to ensure basic housing is affordable. Landlords and estate agents regularly ignore these price controls and grossly inflate the price of accommodation. Tenants are frequently unaware of their right to a rent reduction/fee refund or are too afraid to take action for fear of eviction, paying thousand of euro extra per year in overpriced rent.

Rent Buster NL aims to inform and help as many of these tenants as possible. If you received a letter from me, then you likely are overpaying. Check out our FAQ for more information or send a message for a free consultation.

How we work

If you received a letter from RentBuster and are convinced that you might be overpaying, Rent Buster NL will present your case to the Landlord and negotiate on your behalf for a reduced rent. This is usually not a required step in some cases but it is helpful to seek a diplomatic solution before approaching the Huurcommissie.

Should the landlord fail to reply to respond to our request or if you disagree with his/her counter-offer or if you just want to go straight into it, we will proceed with our case with the Huurcommissie. Rent Buster will take care any paperwork/costs with the Huurcommissie and keep you updated about any developments.

Though I try my best to determine the correct approximate rent price prior to our contact email, it may be first necessary to perform an independent measurement/evaluation of your apartment/room/studio to accurately calculate the rent price particularly if it is close to the liberialization border. I will also need to review your contract to complete the application.

In the event of a favourable judgement by the Huurcommissie, the landlord will be compelled to reduce your rent AND refund you for anything you over-paid since the beginning of your contract/application.
